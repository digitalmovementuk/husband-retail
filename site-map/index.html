Every keyword we researched, with its real Semrush volume, difficulty and buyer intent — and the page it maps to. Sorted by opportunity: highest-intent first.

The headline

Almost nobody searches for these services — even the broadest term, "retail consultant", gets just 20 searches a month in Hong Kong, Singapore, Dubai and Saudi. That's normal for an ultra-premium niche, and it's why we don't chase traffic. But difficulty is low, so when a buyer does search, these pages rank fast — and they also exist to get cited by AI and convert the 149 sites that already link to Husband. Volume isn't the point; being the page that's there when it matters is.
