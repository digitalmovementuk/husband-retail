LOCATION

Located at the heart of the city

LANDMARK is located in Central, the heart of the city and Hong Kong’s primary central business district. Our array of luxury shops and restaurants are spread across four buildings within the same distinguished area, all interlinked by covered walkways: LANDMARK ATRIUM, LANDMARK ALEXANDRA, LANDMARK CHATER and LANDMARK PRINCE’S. Whether you are travelling from the airport, taking a ferry or simply using the MTR or a taxi, LANDMARK can be reached with ease from many places in the city. 


Address: 15 Queen's Road Central, LANDMARK, Central, Hong Kong
Opening Hours: 7am – 12pm daily
